Keith

Hey ya’ll. I am Keith Worsham, a native of Georgia and have spent my entire life in the picturesque landscapes of the Peach State. I was born and raised amidst the rolling hills and lush greenery of the pines and developed a deep passion for the outdoors from a young age. Today, I am a proud owner of a successful landscape design, build, install and maintenance firm servicing the metro Atlanta area.

When I’m not crafting landscapes, I can be found enjoying time with my beautiful bride Pam (of 32 years Sept. 21st). I enjoy the serenity of the Georgia wilderness and traveling with my bride. We have 3 adult children, Ashleigh (and her husband Jake), Ryan (and his wife Juli) and Tyler (just graduated from UVU and starting his next adventure in life).

One of my goals is learning something new and starting a new chapter in my professional career. I look forward to the opportunity of meeting new folks, being challenged and learning something new.
