Rivers of Rapid Rascals Rafting

Welcome to the rivers of Rapid Rascals Rafting. Explore the rivers we offer and create memories for a lifetime

Chattahoochee River - Ocoee River - Nantahala River

Chattahoochee River

A class I and class II river, be prepared to embark on a gentle river rafting adventure that will soothe your soul while your laughter with friends will echo through the hills. "Shooting the Hooch" is not just a rafting experience, it is a rite of passage down the Chattahoochee river. So grab your friends, gear up and get ready to create memories by "Shooting the Hooch" like the locals through the heartland of the state.

Ocoee River

A class IV and class V river, gather your friends and your courage if you dare descend into the unpredictable twists and turns and embark on a river rafting adventure that will have your heart pounding. Feel the rush of excitement as you hold on for your life as you scream down this river. The Ocoee river, a roaring beast hungry for adventure, challenges you to the end

Nantahala River

A class II and class III river that allows everyone the opportunity to enjoy the rapids. This river will take you through the mountains in a shaded gorge with some of the coldest waters around. with plenty of long runs and short runs, this pushy, rocky adventure is promised to be the best as you end at the Nantahala Falls.
